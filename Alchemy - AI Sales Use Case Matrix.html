→Agentic access lifecycle management where AI requests, grants, and revokes access without tickets
 Speed up clinician access provisioning New clinical staff wait days for system access because provisioning is manual and approval chains are slow. AI automates role-based provisioning tied to HR onboarding workflows.[11]
→Zero-touch onboarding where AI provisions, verifies, and certifies access before day one
 Manage contractor and third-party access External contractors regularly access plant systems with credentials that are rarely reviewed or revoked. AI tracks third-party access continuously and flags dormant or excessive permissions.[10]
→Automated contractor lifecycle management with agentic off-boarding on contract expiration
 Govern machine and non-human identities In SaaS environments, APIs, bots, and service accounts outnumber human users. AI discovers and classifies non-human identities that have never been inventoried, let alone governed.[12]
→Agentic machine identity governance that rotates credentials and enforces least-privilege automatically
 Enforce least-privilege across client environments Consultants often carry broader access than their current engagement requires. AI continuously certifies active permissions against current role and flags excess access for immediate review.[11]

 Secure seasonal workforce access at scale Retail organizations add and remove thousands of seasonal employees every year. Manual provisioning and deprovisioning at that scale creates access gaps that persist long after workers leave.[10]

 Meet zero trust mandates without a large IAM team Federal and state zero trust directives require continuous identity verification that most agencies can't staff manually. AI enforces adaptive authentication policies across all user types without additional headcount.[5]
→Agentic zero trust enforcement that adapts access policies based on real-time risk signals
 Enforce PIV and CAC compliance at scale Managing smart card authentication across large, distributed federal workforces is operationally complex and audit-intensive. AI automates certificate lifecycle management and flags compliance gaps before audits.[5]
→Continuous identity assurance platform with agentic certificate rotation and anomaly detection
